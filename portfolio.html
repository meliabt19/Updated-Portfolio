My Applications

Project 1: Project 1 was a News and the Photo retriver that used the News API and Places API to query it's searches> The Photo retriever would take inputs of keywords, place names, specific locations, types of places, and a distance meter of up to 50,000 meters of your current location. Once the data was inputed it and submitted you would recieve places based off your input that would allow you access to photos people have taken of the place. The News retriever worked off of keywords, which works best with current news articles. Once the keyword was inputed and submitted you would recieve local, state, national, and international news pertaining to your search criteria. This was my first group project as well as the first time I have ever created a more complex website that showed me the improtance of time managament and communication among your developer group.

Day Planner: The Day Planner is an agenda application that allows a user to add daily events to their calendar and in real time tell when they would need to be at their next event. The application uses Eastern Standard Time as the time basis and as it goes through a normal workday (9am-5pm) it blocks the boxes out with colors. Green for boxes with events that are still yet to come, red for events that are in the current hour, and gray for all the unattended boxes with no event for the day.
